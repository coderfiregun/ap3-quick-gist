# High Level System Design (HLD) Course

## Course Snapshot
- **Format:** 5 weeks, 3 live classes per week, 2 hours per class
- **Support:** Post-class doubt assistance and active Discord community
- **Access:** Lifetime recordings of all batches, downloadable notes, and revision quizzes
- **Extras:** Technology demos covering commonly used backend components

## Weekly Roadmap

### Week 1 – Networking, Clients, and Protocols
- Client-server architecture fundamentals
- HTTP, WebSockets, and Server-Sent Events
- Service decomposition: monoliths vs. microservices
- Remote communication patterns with gRPC

### Week 2 – Servers, Scaling, and Caching
- Latency vs. throughput trade-offs
- Consistency, availability, and the CAP theorem
- Sync vs. async communication; pub/sub systems (Kafka, RabbitMQ)
- Proxies, load balancers, request routing, and caching strategies

### Week 3 – Storage, Databases, and Indexing
- Comparing SQL, NoSQL, and specialized storage engines
- Migration strategies, indexing, replication, and sharding
- Consistent hashing and scaling (horizontal vs. vertical)
- Distributed transactions and data durability guarantees

### Week 4 – End-to-End System Design
- Capacity planning, feature estimation, and resource sizing
- Polling vs. streaming data delivery; configuration management
- Rate limiting, pagination/filtering, resiliency, and fault tolerance
- Observability foundations: logging, alerting, metrics, and tracing
- Secure API design and production hardening

### Week 5 – Case Studies and Interview Prep
- Chat platforms (e.g., WhatsApp, Discord)
- Video streaming (e.g., YouTube)
- Ride sharing (e.g., Uber)
- Brokerage platforms (e.g., Zerodha, Upstox)
- Mock interviews, question patterns, and revision playbook

## Deliverables & Resources
- Structured notes and quizzes for spaced repetition
- Architecture diagrams and templates for system design interviews
- Lifetime access to updated content and live cohorts
- Dedicated support channels for follow-up queries
